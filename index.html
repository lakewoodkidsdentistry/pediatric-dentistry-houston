FAQs About Pediatric Preventative Dentistry in Houston

Q: When should my child start preventative dental visits?
A: Ideally by their first birthday or within six months after the first tooth erupts.

Q: Are fluoride treatments safe for children?
A: Yes! Fluoride is proven to be safe and highly effective in cavity prevention when applied professionally.

Q: What is the purpose of dental sealants?
A: Sealants act as a barrier to protect molars from food and bacteria that lead to cavities.

Q: How often should my child visit the dentist?
A: Every six months for regular checkups and cleanings is ideal.
